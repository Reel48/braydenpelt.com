# CLAUDE.md

Personal website built with Next.js 16, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS 4 (pnpm).

## Content

Editable text content lives in typed data files under `content/` and renders
through components in `components/cards/` and the page files under `app/`.

**When writing or editing any user-facing copy, follow [STYLE.md](STYLE.md)** —
the site's content follows the Chicago Manual of Style. In particular: closed
(unspaced) em dashes, curly quotes, *italic* (`*…*`) for titles of works in
prose, and small numbers spelled out in running text.
